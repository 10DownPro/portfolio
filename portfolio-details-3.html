ANA Mechanical — A Clean, Conversion-Focused Redesign

ANA Mechanical’s previous website was cluttered and difficult to navigate. I led a complete redesign using WordPress and Elementor, focusing on creating a user-friendly layout that better highlights services and encourages conversions.

The updated design simplified booking, showcased testimonials, and incorporated structured service pages — resulting in a faster, modern site that better reflects the client’s professionalism.
