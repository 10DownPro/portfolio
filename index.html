About

My love for tech started long before I ever worked in it. As a kid, I was surrounded by LEGO sets, taking apart gadgets, and rebuilding things just to see how they worked. Those early moments taught me how to think in systems and how small pieces connect to create something bigger. I didn’t have access to coding classes or fancy equipment. I had curiosity, imagination, and a determination to figure things out. That curiosity still drives me today.

Today, I’m a Technical Project Manager, Systems & AI Engineer, and Founder of Tech Teens Inc., a nonprofit empowering youth with hands on experience in AI, cybersecurity, and coding. My path into tech wasn’t traditional. I taught myself web design, joined a coding bootcamp, and landed a Systems Admin role before graduating. Since then, I’ve built infrastructure from the ground up, automated complex workflows, and launched scalable programs that connect technology, process, and people.

I bring a blend of systems operations, project delivery, and automation strategy, grounded in over five years of technical experience and more than a decade of leadership. I enjoy translating technical complexity into simple, human centered solutions through thoughtful process improvement, collaboration, and a mission driven mindset.

Right now, I focus on designing AI powered systems, improving operational efficiency, and leading initiatives that make technology more accessible. That includes automating workflows for enterprise platforms and mentoring teens so they can see themselves as future innovators.
