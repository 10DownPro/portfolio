About

My love for tech started long before I ever worked in it. As a kid, I was surrounded by LEGO sets, taking apart gadgets, & rebuilding things just to understand how they worked. Those early moments taught me how to think in systems — how small pieces connect to create something bigger.

I didn’t grow up with access to coding classes or expensive equipment. What I had was curiosity, imagination, & the determination to figure things out. That mindset still drives the way I build today.

Now, I work at the intersection of operations, automation, technical execution, & people-centered technology. I’ve supported enterprise systems, built AI-powered workflows, led technical initiatives, & founded Tech Teens Inc. — a nonprofit helping youth gain real-world experience in tech.

My path into tech wasn’t traditional. I taught myself web design, joined a coding bootcamp, & landed a Systems Administrator role before graduating. Since then, I’ve built infrastructure, streamlined operations, automated workflows, & designed systems that help teams move smarter.

I work best where things are messy: unclear processes, disconnected tools, scattered communication, manual workflows, & ideas that need structure. I bring the technical understanding to build the system, the project mindset to move it forward, & the leadership experience to keep people aligned.
