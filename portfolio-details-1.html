Royalty Essentials Co — A Calming, Client-Focused Experience

The client wanted a warm, modern design that reflected the healing nature of her work while simplifying the appointment process. I focused on balance — using soft visuals, clear layouts, and integrated booking to make scheduling easy and stress-free.

The site includes third-party booking and contact integration, allowing seamless client communication and service management directly from the site.

My Role: Lead Designer — Brand Design, UX/UI, Web Development, and Booking Integration.
