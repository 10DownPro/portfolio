Tech Teens Inc — Empowering Youth Through Technology

Tech Teens Inc. is a youth nonprofit dedicated to making tech education accessible to underrepresented communities. I designed and developed the organization’s full website — built to reflect a mission-driven brand and simplify engagement for students, parents, & sponsors.

The site features an energetic, modern interface with clear calls to action, structured program layouts, and donation-friendly design. It now serves as the digital hub for program sign-ups, volunteer recruitment, and partner outreach.

My Role: Founder & Lead Designer — Brand strategy, UX/UI design, content structure, and web development.

Stack: WordPress | Elementor | HTML | CSS | Google Analytics
